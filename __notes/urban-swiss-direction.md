# Zennyx Urban Swiss Direction

Primary direction: `/`

Reference route: `/urban-swiss` (private and noindexed)

## Core idea

Urban Swiss combines the clarity of the International Typographic Style with the energy of city posters, wayfinding, pasted notices, oversized route numbers, and imperfect physical placement.

Zennyx uses this system as a creative game studio, not as a design portfolio. The game project, fictional world, playtest status, devlogs, and studio crew remain the primary content.

## Visual language

- Oversized route numbers and vertical indices
- Asymmetric poster compositions
- Strong color fields using the existing green, amber, ink, and neutral palette
- Cropped game titles and directional arrows
- Development notes arranged like a poster wall
- Hard edges without SaaS-style cards or pill controls
- Sans-serif structure with selective human serif accents

## Separation from Fajar Works

- No warm cream and rust-led portfolio identity
- No full-page fine grid
- No portfolio case-study screenshots or capability matrices
- No all-uppercase personal positioning hero
- No work/skills/archive framing
- Zennyx leads with the current game, Andara, build notes, playable scope, and the two-person studio

## Anti-AI UI rules

- No glassmorphism, glow, gradient mesh, floating 3D objects, or generated illustration
- No bento dashboard, fake analytics, metrics, or product feature cards
- No interchangeable rounded sections
- No generic startup promises
- Motion should feel like poster shifts or wayfinding transitions, not app-dashboard animation
